I-CBB: I See Bacterial Blight

Prototype for our Thesis

For our undergraduate study, we conducted a study on using machine learning and computer vision to detect probable bacterial blight infested cassava leaves. As such, we developed a mobile application that utilizes mobile camera or asks permission to access files to scan through cassava leaf images.

Proactively, I designed the initial interface of the mobile application.

For our final project in Data Mining, our course elective, we developed a system integrated with machine learning to automatically classify books by their genre. In addition, data analytics was done to determine frequently used keywords.

In this collaborative project, I did UI/UX research to formulate designs for the system and collaborated with the UI/UX theme to wireframe and build a prototype using Figma.
