i-CBB (I See Bacterial Blight)
UI/UX Design / Front-End & Mobile Developement / Collaboration (Jan Nicole Casillan, M.C, J.F.O, J.T)
Background For our undergraduate study, we conducted a study on using machine learning and computer vision to detect probable bacterial blight infested cassava leaves. As such, we developed a mobile application that utilizes mobile camera or asks permission to access files to scan through cassava leaf images. I proactively researched for detection application designs and created the initial interface of the mobile application.
Objective. Develop a mobile application that can detect probable infected cassava leaves with bacterial blight. For such, detection models have also be developed and fine-tuned that was able to produce a high accuracy and reliable to give accurate results to data or images of cassava leaves outside of the dataset.
