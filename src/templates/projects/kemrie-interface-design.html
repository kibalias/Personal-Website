Kemrie Barcenas

A Music Artist's Website

Navigating Music and Persona of a Rising Star

ROLE

UI/UX Designer, Front-end Developer

TOOLS

Figma, Photoshop, HTML, JavaScript, TailwindCSS, VueJS

TEAM

Collaboration with C.M.B

BACKGROUND

As part of the final output for our internship, our supervisor assigned us to create a UI/UX design for Kemrie Barcenas. This is a work by pair and I spearheaded the utilization of technologies for this project and provided support in troubleshooting of the dependencies used. Unlike the former tasks, we have been given a color palette to work on. Regularly, feedbacks are given and the design is updated based from these.

OBJECTIVES

  • Create a music artist website and design for Kemrie Barcenas featuring her new songs and albums, a gallery for her images, a blog page, and a contact page that contains her business email and social media links.
  • Development of a user friendly admin page or content management page for non-tech savvy users where the music artist or her management can easily update the contents of her website - images, blogs, songs, albums, etc.
