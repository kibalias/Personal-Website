KEMRIE BARCENAS
UI/UX Design / Front-End Development / Collaboration (Jan Nicole Casillan, C.M.B)
Background. As part of the final output for our internship, our supervisor assigned us to create a UI/UX design for Kemrie Barcenas. This is a work by pair and I spearheaded the utilization of technologies for this project and provided support in troubleshooting of the dependencies used. Unlike the former tasks, we have been given a color palette to work on. Regularly, feedbacks are given and the design is updated based from these.
Objective. Create a music artist website for Kemrie Barcenas featuring her new songs and albums, a gallery for her images, a blog page, and a contact page that contains her business email and social media links. A user friendly admin page or content management page for non-tech savvy users should also be included and developed where the music artist or her management can easily update the contents of her website - images, blogs, songs, albums, etc.
