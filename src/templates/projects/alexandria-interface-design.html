Alexandria: Automatic Book Classifier
UI/UX Design / Front-End & Back-End Development / Machine Learning / Collaboration (Jan Nicole Casillan, J.P.M, J.F.O, J.T, M.J.A, J.M.P, B.C,)
Background. An online book inventory system integrated with machine learning to automatically classify the genre of books to ease the job of librarians or library staffs and provide a system that provides standardized genre tags and headings.
Objective. Librarian, staff, or bookkeepersâ€™ experience in record management can be improved as e-libraries can utilize the trends in information technology, specifically, machine learning. With the plethora of books, they are burdened with the loads of books that they have to categorize traditionally and based on their subjective experience. Thus, this project automates the classification of books by their genres with less intervention by humans. Other than automation, this also explores and discovers the frequent keywords or most used terms on each genre involved in this project.
